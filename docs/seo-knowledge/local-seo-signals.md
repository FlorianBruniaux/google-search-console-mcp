# Local SEO Ranking Signals

Source: adapted from claude-seo local SEO references (MIT). Data points from
Whitespark Local Search Ranking Factors 2026, BrightLocal Local Consumer Review
Survey 2025, and Sterling Sky field research.

## Google Business Profile signals (primary factors)

These dominate local pack and local finder results:

- **GBP primary category**: strongest single signal for local pack eligibility.
  Picking the wrong primary category can outweigh all other optimization.
- **Business name relevance**: keyword in business name still provides a lift,
  but keyword stuffing in the name violates Google guidelines.
- **Review quantity and recency**: Whitespark 2026 ranks review count and recency
  among the top 5 local pack factors. Recency matters because the "18-day rule"
  (Sterling Sky) suggests Google weights reviews from the past 18 days most heavily
  in freshness scoring.
- **Review response rate**: responding to reviews, including negative ones,
  correlates with local pack prominence.

## On-page and website signals

- NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone) across the website, GBP, and citations.
- Localized content with city/neighborhood mentions in title tags and H1.
- Schema markup: `LocalBusiness` (or subtype) with address, phone, opening hours,
  geo coordinates. Missing `aggregateRating` is a common gap.
- Page speed: slow-loading location pages lose local pack visibility on mobile.

## Citation and link signals

- BrightLocal LCRS 2025: citation volume from authoritative local directories
  (Yelp, Apple Maps, Bing Places, industry-specific) remains a top-20 signal.
- Local links (chamber of commerce, local news, sponsorships) carry more weight
  than generic links for local pack ranking.

## AI-enhanced local search (2025-2026)

- Google's AI Overviews surface for high-intent local queries ("best X near me").
  Businesses with complete GBP profiles and structured review data appear more
  consistently in AI Overview citations.
- Local inventory ads integrated into AI-driven shopping panels increase local
  conversion for product-based businesses.
